Our Mission

At Rockstar Bookkeeping & Accounting, our mission is to put your finances in the spotlight. We provide online, affordable, and efficient subscription-based bookkeeping services designed to empower every business — from solo artists to full ensembles.

We go beyond traditional bookkeeping to handle every detail of your back-office operations. With meticulous attention and comprehensive financial analysis, we streamline your books and equip you with the insights to make decisions that drive your business forward.

FAQ
Why should I outsource my bookkeeping? +
Outsourcing is more efficient and cost-effective than maintaining an in-house bookkeeper. You get expert services — including payroll, invoicing, and financial reporting — for a fraction of the price, with a dedicated account manager always available.
Is there a long-term commitment? +
We work month-to-month. Cancel anytime if you're not 100% satisfied — no questions asked.
How is pricing calculated? +
Pricing is based on your plan tier plus the number of monthly transactions and invoices. Each plan includes a base amount, with add-ons available above that threshold.
What software do you use? +
We integrate with all major platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, Wave, and more. Our bookkeepers are experienced across the industry's most widely-used tools.
Can you help with back taxes and catch-up bookkeeping? +
Yes! We can clean up old books, organize historical records, and guide you through back taxes and any penalties — with proven systems that have helped hundreds of clients in the same position.
